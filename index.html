About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at École des Mines de Saint-Étienne, working under the supervision of Dr. Antoine Zimmermann and Dr. Victor Charpenay. My research focuses on Knowledge Graph Embeddings and their applications in Semantic Web technologies.

Previously, I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science at University X, where I worked on recommendation systems algorithms and explainable AI. My academic journey has been driven by a passion for data mining and artificial intelligence.
